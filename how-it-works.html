Frequently asked

The questions every business asks.

What if I never switch automation on?

Your website stays yours, fully functional, forever. The automation layer is built in but inactive. You owe nothing, and we part as friends whenever you choose.

Can I take my website with me if I leave?

Absolutely. You own the site, the code, the content, and your domain. We provide a full export on request — no lock-in, no drama.

What if the automation doesn’t bring growth?

Phase 3 payments only trigger on the growth metrics we agree together, in writing, before activation. If the lift doesn’t happen, nothing charges.

How much does Phase 2 onboarding cost?

It depends on your business, your systems, and the scope of automation. That’s what the free discovery call is for — to understand exactly what you need, then agree a fair, fixed number together.

Who owns the AI agents?

Dreams of Grace owns the agent framework and system design. They’re licensed to you for use in your business. This protects both of us and keeps the partnership clean.

How do you protect my data?

We follow Australian Privacy Principles and GDPR-aligned practices. Your data stays yours, always. Your dashboard shows every action taken — no black boxes.

How long does it take to build my site?

From brief to live, typically within two weeks for most businesses. The timeline is part of what we agree together after discovery.

What happens if I want to pause automation later?

You can pause or switch off anytime. Your website keeps running. Any active Phase 3 value-windows complete on the terms we agreed, and we sort it out fairly — no hostage-taking.
