← Back to home Our promise

Grace, iron-clad.

Every partnership we build rests on these promises. Written in plain English before anything else is agreed — because trust is the foundation, not the afterthought.

To you, our partner

What we promise you.

These are the commitments we put into every agreement. They’re not marketing — they’re the floor beneath our partnership.

🔒 Your data stays yours

We process your data to run the services you’ve asked for — never to sell, trade, or train anything without your say-so. You can export everything at any time. We follow Australian Privacy Principles and GDPR-aligned practices.

🏰 Your site is always yours

Your website belongs to you from day one. Code, content, and domain. If automation is switched off or paused, your site continues to work fully. You are never locked in.

💎 Our fee is only when you win

Phase 2 onboarding is agreed up front, in writing, after discovery. Phase 3 payments only trigger on measurable growth we agree together. If the value doesn’t come, the charge doesn’t either.

💸 Fair refund

If we don’t deliver what was agreed on Phase 1 or Phase 2, we make it right. That might mean a rebuild, a refund, or a release — whatever keeps grace in the partnership.

📊 Full transparency

Your dashboard shows every agent action, every result, every metric. No black boxes, no hidden logic. If you can’t see it, we won’t do it.

⚖️ Your legal rights, fully preserved

Nothing in our agreement displaces any of your rights under Australian Consumer Law or UK consumer law. Our promises are in addition to your legal protections, never in place of them.

🗝️ No lock-in, ever

You can take your website, your data, and your agreement with you. Thirty-day notice to wind things down. We hand over cleanly, with a smile and a prayer.

🤝 Written first, then built

Nothing activates, nothing charges, until every detail is agreed in a written scope. If it’s not in the document, it doesn’t happen.

🔗 Transparent plug-in costs

The two tiny running costs every website needs — your domain name and hosting — are paid directly to the providers, not to us. We set it all up for free, point you to the most honest low-cost option (often free on starter tiers), and never take a cent of those fees.

Our word

The Price-Match & Fix-Match promise.

You deserve the best. If we’re not already it, we’ll become it.

Found it fairly priced elsewhere?

Show us. We’ll review openly and match it where the scope is truly equal. No ego. No pressure.

Cheaper quote missing features?

We’ll show you the gap side-by-side — in plain English, no jargon — so you can choose clearly.

A competitor has a better idea?

We’ll build it into your site, free. The goal is your win — not our pride.

We can’t deliver what we promised?

We make it right. Every time. That’s the whole point.

What we ask of you

What we need from you.

Grace flows both ways. Here’s what keeps the partnership strong on your side:

  • Honesty about your business. The more we know, the better we build. Nothing leaves our systems without consent.
  • Timely feedback. We move fast. Quick, clear feedback keeps us both moving.
  • Kept agreements. When Phase 2 or Phase 3 is agreed in writing, we honour it together.
  • Respect for the craft. Unlimited revisions, not unlimited rework. We listen hard and iterate with care.
  • Good faith. We treat every partner with grace. We ask the same in return.
The hard truth

Where our IP stands.

Dreams of Grace builds and owns the automation agents, the dashboard systems, the phase-trigger framework, and the integration methodology. These are licensed to you for use in your business — that licence protects both of us. Your content, your data, your domain, and your website design remain fully yours.

This keeps the partnership clean: you own what you bring and what we build for you. We own the underlying systems that make the magic possible. That balance is what lets us keep giving websites away for free.
